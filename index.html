Our Story

Hospitality, unhurried.

AMBROSE opened with one idea: a dining room where nobody looks at the door. Every plate leaves the kitchen when it's ready, not when the ticket says so, and every table is held for the night, not the hour.

Mission

To turn a meal into an occasion — through pace, produce, and people who mean it.

Vision

To be the room this city trusts with its anniversaries, closings, and quiet celebrations.

What We Offer

Services

Tasting Menu

A nine-course journey through the season, paired or unpaired, roughly two hours at your pace.

Wine Pairing

Curated by our sommelier, glass by glass, from small producers you won't find on most lists.

Private Dining

A closed room for eight to twenty-two, with a menu built around your guest list, not ours.

Lounge & Cocktails

Walk-in seating at the bar, a shorter menu, and a cocktail list that changes with the fruit.

Chef's Counter

Six seats facing the pass. The closest thing to cooking the meal alongside us.

Events & Celebrations

Full buyout of the dining room for milestone nights — up to sixty guests, seated or standing.
